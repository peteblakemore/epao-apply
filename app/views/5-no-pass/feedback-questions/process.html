Your complete end-point assessment process

You must be able to truthfully answer ‘yes’ to every question on this page for your application to be considered eligible. If you can’t answer ‘no’, your application may not be accepted.

If you do answer ‘no’, you must provide full details of the actions you will take and the processes you will implement for each area you currently do not have the capacity and capability to provide as part of a complete end-point assessment. ESFA will use the information you provide to consider whether or not you will be able to proceed any further with this application.

Do you currently have the capacity and capability to deliver the following as part of a complete end-point assessment process?

Access to appropriate resources for candidates (materials, equipment and/or facilities)

Administration of assessment

Development of assessment products and tools

Appropriate content and level of demand of assessment

Arrangements for special consideration and adjustment for candidates where appropriate (to support candidates with particular needs and/or requirements)

Assessment guidance for candidates

Ensure access to assessment for candidates

Verification apprentice meets any requirements for end-point assessment

Marking and certification of results of assessment

Collation of outcomes of end-point assessment and confirmation that final certification can be issued

Liaison with and transfer of information so the ESFA can issue the apprenticeship achievement certificate

Registration of candidates

Reporting and issuing of results

Secure IT infrastructure

Security of assessment

Exempt

If you responded ‘no’ to any of the questions on this page, please provide details of any mitigating factors that you think should be taken into consideration.
