Required documentation

You must be able to truthfully answer yes to every question under ‘Required documentation’ for your application to be considered eligible. If you can’t answer yes, your application may not be accepted.

If you do answer ‘no’, you must provide full details of any remedial action that you think the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) should take into consideration. ESFA will use the information you provide to consider whether or not you will be able to proceed any further with this application.
